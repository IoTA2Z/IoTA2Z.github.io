San Francisco, California

Tivix is headquartered in San Francisco, and its the nexus from which weve grown our team in the US and internationally. Our core team here has battle-proven Silicon Valley expertise. Located in the beating heart of tech, Tivix SF draws some of the best engineering talent in the world.

2845 California Street San Francisco, CA 94115 connect@tivix.com

Portland, Oregon

Tivix has an office in the beautiful Pacific Northwest, home to such companies as Nike, Microsoft, F5 Networks, and Expedia. When not drinking craft beer and exploring Portlands many bridges, the engineering-focused Portland team are hard at work delivering rock-solid software to some of our most prominent US clients.

3822 SW Corbett Ave Portland, OR 97239 connect@tivix.com

New York, New York

Founded in 2014, our outpost in New York prides itself on its diverse team of developers, designers and project managers. With a timezone that facilitates communication between the West Coast and Europe, our NYC office brings a broad skillset and wealth of experience to engagements across the globe.

11 Park Place New York, NY 10007 connect@tivix.com

London, United Kingdom

Tivix London is located in South Bank. Opened in 2015, its been an exciting journey for our UK team, as weve seen the city emerge as an increasingly important center of technology and innovation. The newest Tivix office boasts international team members with a range of expertise in our core practice areas.

22 Upper Ground London SE1 9PD United Kingdom connect@tivix.com

Wrocaw, Poland

Our Polish office is in Wrocaw, a beautiful university town in Western Poland (180 miles from Berlin). It is home to the Wrocaw University of Technology, one of Europes top-ranked technology universities. Overseen by Dariusz Fryta, Tivix Wrocaw connects the cream of Polands tech talent with leading brands worldwide.
