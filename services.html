CLIENT SECTORS

Tivix works across a variety of industries. Our clients are a diverse mix of Fortune 500 firms, venture-backed startups and innovative government organizations/NGOs.

GOVERNMENT/NGO

We build software for humanitarian agencies and governments. Projects include platforms to relieve hunger in famine-stricken areas and knowledge platforms for disseminating research. The UN's World Food Programme, the European Commision and UNICEF have all contracted with Tivix to build web and mobile applications for their organizations.

LIFE SCIENCES/MEDICAL

Tivix builds life science applications for large enterprises, startups and everything in between. Our seasoned engineering team has the chops to develop accurate and high-performing scientific software that performs to the exacting standards of the medical and scientific communities.

CONSUMER

We create consumer-facing mobile, web and cloud apps that are a joy to use. Embedded into our development ethos is a focus on user experience and visual design that puts the end-user first. Our team delivers consumer products that are fast, responsive and intuitive.

FINTECH/FINANCIAL SERVICES

Fintech is rapidly disrupting the financial services industry as we know it. At Tivix, we're leading the charge. We specialize in FinTech and financial services application development, having built the software behind hedge funds, crowdfunding platforms, and consumer lending firms.
