Software Architecture and Development Process

Complex IoT application challenges and an endless number of potential technology choices and approaches necessitates a tailor-made software architecture design for each of your projects. While most IoT projects involve both edge and centralized (usually cloud-based) software components, the similarities between any two projects can quickly diverge from there.

IOTA2Z takes a holistic and informed approach in our architecture design. Our expertise spans horizontally scalable cloud applications, microservice architectures, public and private cloud infrastructures, distributed systems, asynchronous messaging, and web and mobile-based user interfaces. We are expert in multiple object oriented and functional languages, standard application platforms and integration tools, relevant standards, and mainstream design patterns. IOTA2Z further specializes in complex web application development for our fortune 500, startup, military, and governmental customers. We reduce development risk for our clients by using Agile methodologies and standards-based code to get applications to production quickly.

Usability

Software users have become accustomed to beautiful, logical interfaces, in both enterprise and consumer applications. In IoT applications specifically, advanced features such as “zero touch” provisioning techniques are also a necessity. IOTA2Z delivers these for our clients through a process of design thinking, user research and assumption testing. We design with usability in mind from day one, building applications with bullet-proof provisioning, intuitive user experiences, and fast response times.

Scalability

Need an app that can scale to millions of IoT endpoints and/or users in a matter of months? You’ve found the right team. We build custom IoT apps from the ground up that are designed with massive scale as a principal priority. We are experts at developing apps that effectively and cost-efficiently scale with your business.

Architecture

In general, IOTA2Z embraces microservice architectures and service oriented architectures (SOA), design patterns based on discrete pieces of software providing application functionality as services to other applications. This is particularly important in a world where most IoT applications require 24x7 highly available operation, frequent functionality upgrades, and massive scale.

Continuous Delivery and Agile Iteration

We manage all our projects with a highly iterative, continuous delivery, continuous feedback approach. Living and breathing Agile methodologies and practices ensures that we optimize project resources, minimize project risks, hit deliverable milestones, and meet or exceed customer expectations. Further, our use of current DevOps practices and continuous integration/delivery enable us to accelerate product delivery and simplify sustaining engineering over an applications full lifecycle.
