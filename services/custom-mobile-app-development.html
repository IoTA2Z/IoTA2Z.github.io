Custom Mobile App Development

Mobile continues to rapidly change the business landscape, both for internal B2B initiatives and user-facing B2C apps. Our expertise in custom mobile app development gives our clients the opportunity to pack incredible functionality in the palm of a user's hand. All the native mobile apps we build deliver a world-class user experience, with intuitive functionality, gorgeous design and powerful features.

Experts across platforms

Our clients need mobile apps that perform across different device models and operating systems. And our team delivers for them. We’re experts at using Swift and Objective-C to build native iOS apps and Java for native Android development. Our mobile engineering expertise is complemented by the work of our UX designers. Mobile experiences need a well-thought out user interface and experience to succeed. To ensure this, the Tivix design team works closely with our developers from the outset, using data-informed research to ship mobile products that delight users.

We help clients win in the mobile-first era

The ability to reach consumers at different touch points is essential in modern business. In the digital era, businesses and brands that haven’t embraced mobile are missing out. Cross-device business strategies are ushering in a new era of customer engagement and retention that is increasingly being played out on mobile. Our experience across industries helps us not only to build mobile products, but to guide go-to-market strategy to help these products succeed.
