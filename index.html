Our Process

From the first contact with IoT to introduce the solution to you.

Go your way with us. Together with you we develop individual, tailor-made IoT applications including hardware and software.

  • CONSULTATION

    Get to know the Internet of Things. We advise you on the technology selection.

  • Design

    Conceive with us your application solution, from the sensor to the mobile application or the integration into your system landscape.

  • IMPLEMENTATION

    Proof of concept, pilot or product launch, we develop hardware and software for you.

  • GETTING STARTED

    Implementation planning, training and installation work from a single source. This is how the start in the Internet of Things succeeds.
